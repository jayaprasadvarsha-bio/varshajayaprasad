Sephora Middle East
Paid Social Campaign Concept
The brief

Build brand love and drive footfall to UAE stores during Ramadan, a high-consideration gifting period with a distinct cultural tone that mass beauty advertising consistently gets wrong.

The approach

Shift the creative register from aspirational to intimate. Short-form video content centred on the gifting ritual, not the product. Collaborate with GCC micro-creators whose audiences trust them. Run paid amplification behind organic top-performers rather than leading with produced content.

How I use data

Analytical thinking

Numbers tell you what happened. The question is always why, and what to do next. Here are examples of how I translate platform data into creative decisions.

YouTube Analytics
Audience retention drops sharply at the 0:45 mark

Across multiple channels I noticed a consistent pattern: retention held well through the first 40 seconds then dropped steeply before the one minute mark, regardless of content topic.

Restructured intros to front-load the core value proposition within the first 30 seconds, and moved scene changes to occur before the 45 second threshold. Retention improved across affected channels.
Meta Insights
Reach high, engagement rate below benchmark

A client account was hitting strong reach numbers on boosted posts but seeing engagement rates consistently below industry benchmarks. The creative was polished but static.

Shifted creative direction toward native-feeling formats including text-led posts and short video over produced graphics. Engagement rate increased within two posting cycles.
TikTok Analytics
Strong views, low profile visits and follows

Content was being discovered through the For You page but not converting viewers into followers. This usually signals a hook-content mismatch, where the video attracts a broad audience that does not match the account's actual niche.

Recommended tightening the content brief to serve a more specific audience over chasing broad reach. Niche relevance converts to follows; generic reach does not.
